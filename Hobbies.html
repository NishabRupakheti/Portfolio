Hobbies

Gaming

Gaming is a big deal for me – it's where I dive into these awesome virtual worlds full of adventures and challenges. I'm all about that fast-paced action in games like Valorant, where you gotta think strategically and move quick. Then there's GTA – those open worlds are like my playground, with high-speed chases and epic heists keeping things thrilling. And don't get me started on Assassin's Creed - I love getting lost in those historical and fantastical stories, time-traveling through different eras. Beyond just being a fun time, gaming helps me chill out and work on my problem-solving skills while navigating through all kinds of virtual landscapes.

Coding

I really enjoy messing around with coding in my free time. It's like this cool mix of brainy challenges and creative fun. I'm especially into building websites – there's something satisfying about bringing a digital idea to life. I use tech like React, Bootstrap, and JavaScript to jazz up my projects and make them look slick. It's not just about showing off my coding skills; it's also about creating websites that are both smooth to use and easy on the eyes. The whole problem-solving aspect and the nitty-gritty of coding give me this awesome way to express myself. So yeah, coding's not just a hobby for me; it's legit good times in the tech world.

Taking a walk

Sometimes, I need a break from screens and coding, you know? So, one of my hobbies is just taking a walk. It's nice to step away from the virtual world and get some fresh air. I love how it gives me a chance to relax, clear my mind, and enjoy the real world for a bit. It's a simple but awesome way for me to balance out all the tech stuff and appreciate the simple things around me.

Movies and TV series

I have a deep passion for the world of cinema and television, and my favorite pastime revolves around watching an extensive variety of movies and TV series. From the captivating fantasy of "House of the Dragon" to the gritty allure of "Peaky Blinders" and the mind-bending intrigue of "Mr. Robot," I relish exploring diverse genres and storylines. Animated TV shows like "Family Guy" add a delightful touch of humor to my viewing experience. Additionally, I am a devoted follower of iconic movie franchises such as "Mission Impossible," the suave world of James Bond, the epic Marvel Cinematic Universe, the mutant adventures of X-Men, and the relentless pursuit of justice in the Terminator series. The cinematic realm, with its rich narratives and visually stunning spectacles, serves as a constant source of entertainment and fascination in my life.
